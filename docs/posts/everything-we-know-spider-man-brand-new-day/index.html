Everything We Know About 'Spider-Man: Brand New Day' — The Web-Slinger's Grittiest Chapter Yet

From Punisher team-ups to a forgotten Peter Parker's mysterious new powers, here's your full behind-the-scenes breakdown of Marvel's most anticipated 2026 sequel.

1

Tom Holland Is Back — But Nobody in New York Remembers Him

Picking up four years after 'No Way Home,' Peter is living completely alone after erasing himself from the memories of everyone he loves. He's now devoted himself full-time to being Spider-Man, patrolling a city that has no idea who he really is.

2

Destin Daniel Cretton Takes Over the Director's Chair

The 'Shang-Chi' filmmaker replaced Jon Watts after being the studios' top choice by September 2024. Kevin Feige has teased Cretton is bringing to life '8 or 9 comic covers' pinned up in his art department, promising the most comics-accurate Spidey movie yet.

3

Peter Teams Up With... The Punisher?!

Jon Bernthal's Frank Castle joins the fray as Marvel pushes Spider-Man into street-level crime instead of world-ending stakes. Feige explained the pairing makes sense since Punisher was created in the pages of a Spider-Man comic, and Holland says the pair's dynamic evolves into a 'big brother/little brother rivalry.'

4

Mark Ruffalo's Hulk Shows Up as an Unlikely Mentor

Bruce Banner steps in to help Peter understand a strange physical transformation his body is undergoing. Ruffalo's casting was only confirmed once filming began in August 2025, after rumors had swirled for months.

5

Sadie Sink's Mystery Character Has Fans Losing Their Minds

The 'Stranger Things' star's role remains officially unconfirmed, with rumors ranging from Jean Grey to Mayday Parker to Gwen Stacy. A hooded figure teased across trailers has only fueled speculation that she's connected to the film's hidden main villain.

6

A Rogues' Gallery of Villains Is Assembling

Michael Mando returns as Mac Gargan, this time fully transformed into Scorpion, joined by Marvin Jones III as crime boss Tombstone. Rumors also point to Boomerang, Tarantula, and Ramrod rounding out a lineup the filmmakers designed so Peter could face threats he could realistically take down in a day or two.

7

Filming Wrapped After a London and Scotland Shoot

Production ran from August to December 2025, with location filming in Glasgow and across England plus soundstage work at Pinewood Studios. A minor delay followed an on-set injury to Holland, and additional photography took place in London in spring 2026 to add humor and rework a villain plotline.

8

Where It Fits in the Multiverse Saga

'Brand New Day' arrives July 31, 2026, positioned as the last major standalone release before the Multiverse Saga's finale in 'Avengers: Doomsday' and 'Avengers: Secret Wars.' Producer Amy Pascal has said the film kicks off a brand new trilogy for Peter, meaning it may largely sidestep the multiverse chaos entirely — unless Spidey has a bigger role to play in the Avengers movies still to come.
